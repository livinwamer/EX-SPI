About Satkhira Polytechnic Institute (SPI)

Established in 2002, Satkhira Polytechnic Institute is the sole government mid-level engineering institution in the Satkhira district. Situated in Labsa, Satkhira Sadar Upazila, beside the Jessore-Satkhira Highway, the institute provides technical and vocational education under the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB). It plays a crucial role in producing skilled technical manpower for the national and international job markets.

The sprawling campus covers an area of approximately 2.05 acres. SPI offers a 4-year Diploma-In-Engineering course across six technologies. The academic year is structured around eight semesters, with the final semester dedicated to Industrial Attachment.
